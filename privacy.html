1. Introduction

Welcome to the VoidGifts Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes how VoidGifts ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our gaming platform ("Service") through Telegram or other platforms.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive through our Service. By using VoidGifts, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Key Privacy Principles:
  • We collect only essential information needed to provide our Service
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • You have control over your personal information and can request deletion at any time
  • We are transparent about our data practices

This Privacy Policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms of Service. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not use VoidGifts.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have important rights regarding your personal information. Depending on your location, these rights may include:

6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. To access your data:

  • View your profile and inventory within the VoidGifts app
  • Contact us to request a comprehensive data export
  • We'll provide your data in a structured, commonly used format within 30 days

6.2 Right to Rectification

If your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can:

  • Update your information by changing settings in your Telegram account (as VoidGifts syncs with Telegram)
  • Contact us to correct any inaccuracies in your gameplay data

6.3 Right to Deletion (Right to be Forgotten)

You can request deletion of your personal information. To delete your data:

  1. Stop using VoidGifts
  2. Contact us with your deletion request
  3. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it by law

Note: Deletion is permanent and will result in loss of all virtual items, coins, and progress.

6.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your information in certain circumstances, such as:

  • While we're verifying the accuracy of your data
  • When you contest our legitimate interest in processing your data
  • When processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion

6.5 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a portable format to transfer to another service. We'll provide your data in JSON or CSV format upon request.

6.6 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of processing, including:

  • Processing for direct marketing purposes
  • Processing based on legitimate interests
  • Processing for research or statistical purposes

6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we process your data based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time through:

  • Settings page in VoidGifts
  • Contacting us directly

6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we've violated your privacy rights, you can:

  • Contact us first to resolve the issue
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority
  • Seek legal remedies available under applicable law

6.9 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 13. We'll respond to your request within 30 days.

Verification: For security purposes, we may ask you to verify your identity before responding to requests regarding your personal information.
